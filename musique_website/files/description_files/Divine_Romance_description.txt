<h1>Description</h1>
This is a transcription of the song "Divine Romance" by Phil Wickham. Phil Wickham tunes down his guitar a 1/2 step so that the song is in B. Here the transcription is written in C, so to play in B just tune down your guitar a 1/2 step just like Phil Wickham.

<h5>Key: B major | Tempo: 90</h5>
