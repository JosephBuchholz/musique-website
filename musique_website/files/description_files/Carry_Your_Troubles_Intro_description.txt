<h1>Description</h1>
This is a full transcription of the song "Carry Your Troubles (Intro)" by Jordan Feliz. This the intro song to his album "The River." The song contains an easy fingerstyle guitar part which is great for beginners wanting to learn fingerstyle guitar.

<h5>Key: C major | Tempo: 54</h5>
