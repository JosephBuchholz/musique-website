<h1>Description</h1>
This is a transcription of the guitar part of Jadon Lavik's arrangement of the hymn "I Surrender All" by Judson W. Van DeVenter (lyricist) and Winfield S. Weeden (melody). Note: the guitar is in a different tuning.

<h5>Key: D major | Tempo: 78</h5>
