<h1>Description</h1>
This is a transcription of the guitar parts for MercyMe's arrangement of the hymn "His Eye is on the Sparrow" by Civilla D. Martin. Note: Guitar 1 is in a different tuning (D open) and both guitars use a slide.

<h5>Key: D major | Tempo: 88</h5>
